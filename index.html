About Lucknow

Lucknow

The city of Nawabs, Lucknow is one of the most popular tourist destinations in India and is visited by travellers from all over the world. It is immensely famous for its hospitality and Mughlai cuisine. Hence, it wouldn't be wrong to say that it is one of the most favourite stoppages for foodies in India. From its luscious biryani to delicious tundey kabab, each and every dish is known for its unique flavour. However, the major share of tourists visiting Lucknow include history lovers and seasonal tourists. As it has a history dating back to thousands of years and has gained fame for age-old culture and traditions, it could be found on the bucket list of every traveller. Did you know that Lucknow is one of the happiest cities in the world? If not, then the following interesting facts about Lucknow are for you. Constructed in the year 1881, Hussainabad Clock Tower in Lucknow is said to be the tallest clock tower in the country and has a total height of about 220 ft. It is one of the most beautiful architectural monuments in the city and was inspired by the Big Ben Tower which is located in London. It is located in the close proximity of Rumi Darwaza and is a popular tourist attraction in Luckno
